City Business Ideas

City Business Ideas

1. Solar Energy Power Hub

This center produces electricity using sunlight. It helps reduce power cuts, saves money, and provides clean energy for the entire city.

2. Public Safety & CCTV Monitoring Center

This center watches CCTV cameras across the city to detect crimes, accidents, and emergencies quickly. It keeps people safe and secure.

3. Online Food & Grocery Delivery Service

This service helps people order groceries and food online and get fast delivery at home. It saves time and reduces crowds in shops.

4. Smart Parking System

A system that shows available parking spots using sensors. It avoids traffic jams, saves fuel, and helps drivers find parking easily.

5. Skill Development & Training Center

A center that trains youth and adults in computer skills, technical skills, and communication. It increases job opportunities in the city.

6. Smart Public Transport System

Buses and autos run with GPS tracking, digital tickets, and real-time timings. It makes travelling easier, faster, and safer.
