1. Solar Energy Power Hub
✔ What is the idea? A central solar station supplying clean energy.
✔ How it helps? Reduces bills, clean energy, fewer power cuts.
✔ Implementation: Select site → Install panels → Battery → Supply → IoT monitor.
✔ Algorithms: Sorting, Graph, Prediction, Optimisation.
2. Public Safety & CCTV Monitoring Center
✔ What is the idea? A central CCTV monitoring and alert center.
✔ How it helps? Quick crime detection, emergency support.
✔ Implementation: Install cameras → Send to center → AI detect → Notify police.
✔ Algorithms: Searching, Pattern recognition, Object detection, Clustering.
3. Online Food & Grocery Delivery Service
✔ What is the idea? An app to deliver food & groceries fast.
✔ How it helps? Saves time, convenience.
✔ Implementation: Build app → connect shops → assign delivery → tracking.
✔ Algorithms: Dijkstra, Sorting, Recommendation, Searching.
4. Smart Parking System
✔ What is the idea? App + sensors to show free parking slots.
✔ How it helps? Saves time, avoids traffic.
✔ Implementation: Install sensors → detect status → app shows slots.
✔ Algorithms: Searching, Graph, Prediction, Dynamic programming.
5. Skill Development & Vocational Training Center
✔ What is the idea? A center to teach technical and job skills.
✔ How it helps? Jobs, income growth, rural development.
✔ Implementation: Choose courses → classrooms → training → certification.
✔ Algorithms: Recommendation, Classification, Sorting, Sequencing.
6. Smart Public Transport System
✔ What is the idea? Real-time bus timings & best routes system.
✔ How it helps? Saves time, avoids crowded buses.
✔ Implementation: GPS → app shows location → predict arrival.
✔ Algorithms: Dijkstra, Graph, Scheduling, Prediction.
