1. Public Library

2. Wildlife Sanctuary

  • Problem: Poor animal tracking, poaching risk, unorganized tourism.
  • Smart Business Idea: GPS animal tracking, online safari booking, AI camera monitoring.
  • Algorithm Used: Graph algorithms for tracking, Dijkstra for safe paths, Scheduling for tourist slots.
  • Business Benefits: Safari ticket revenue, eco-tourism income, research funding.

  • Algorithm Used: Shortest Path for ambulance, Prediction algorithms for disease spread, Greedy for garbage collection routes.
  • Business Benefits: Government health projects, private hospital services, waste contracts.
